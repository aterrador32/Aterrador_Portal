// How Resource Access Works

Sign in with your
University Gmail

All batch resources are stored on a shared Google Drive. Access is protected — only accounts with a verified university email domain can view or download files. No registration needed; just use your existing edu mail.

01

Sign in with Google

Automatic Verification
Your email domain is checked instantly. Non-university accounts are blocked immediately.

Browse & Download

// About the Batch

Where Code Meets
Community

Aterrador — meaning "terrifying" in Spanish — is the identity of the 52nd batch of the CSE Department at Jahangirnagar University. We are engineers, problem-solvers, and collaborators pushing the boundaries together.

This portal is our central hub — schedules, resources, teacher contacts, and batch notices. All resources are protected and accessible only to verified batch members via university email.
