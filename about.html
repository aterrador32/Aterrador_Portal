The Name
ATERRADOR
Español · Spanish / a·te·rra·ˈðor /
[ ah-teh-rah-DOR ]
Meaning & Origin

In Spanish, aterrador is an adjective meaning "terrifying" — something that inspires awe, dread, and overwhelming force. Not horror in the dark, but the kind of terror that seizes you when you witness something so formidable it stops you cold.

We chose this name deliberately. Because we do not intend to be comfortable, predictable, or forgettable. We intend to be the kind of batch that makes people feel the weight of our presence — in every classroom, every lab, every line of code we write.

To be aterrador is not to be feared. It is to be impossible to ignore.

We are ATERRADOR — the 52nd batch of Jahangirnagar University and the 32nd batch of the Department of Computer Science & Engineering. We entered these halls in July 2023 — session 2022-23 — not as strangers, but as 120 minds bound by a shared purpose. We are something far more deliberate than a cohort.

Our journey began behind screens — online classes from November 2023 — testing our resolve before we ever stepped on campus. When we finally walked these halls in February 2024, we carried more than books. We carried the weight of a promise — to one another, to those who taught us, to the institution that trusted us with its name.

We did not name ourselves after something comfortable. We named ourselves after something formidable — because that is what we intend to become.

ATERRADOR · CSE 52nd Batch · 2025

We stood together through history — the July Revolution of 2024 reminded us that some things matter more than grades. When Bangladesh rose up, we rose with it. Through curfews and chaos, we protected each other. And in February 2025, we chose a name that carries weight: ATERRADOR. Something that does not whisper.

We are students of computer science. But we are also architects of a culture — one that values rigor, solidarity, creativity, and the quiet conviction that the best version of this department is still ahead of us. And that we will be the ones to build it.

VISION
// Our Purpose

We Are Here
To Redefine What this department is capable of

The CSE department at Jahangirnagar University has produced extraordinary minds for over three decades. We do not take that lightly. But legacy is not a ceiling. It is a floor — the absolute minimum of what we must achieve before we can begin to justify the name we carry.

Our vision is simple and uncompromising: to raise the class of this department to a standard that its own history has not yet seen.

We want the words "JUCSE" to carry a different weight in this country's technology conversation. Not because of our credentials alone — but because of the culture we build: a culture where excellence is the expectation, where knowledge is shared freely, where the weakest student in our batch is still stronger for having been surrounded by all of us.

We believe that a department's reputation is not built by toppers and CGPA charts. It is built by the kind of people a batch collectively becomes — and the mark they leave on everyone they work with, for the rest of their lives.
